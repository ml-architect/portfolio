## About the Project

An interactive educational case study for the [RANEPA LIA](https://lia.ranepa.ru/) platform, in which students go through the full Data Science cycle: from EDA of real data to an ML model and business recommendations.

The domain is retail in the sterile area of Sheremetyevo Airport (SVO). Data covers May--June 2022: retail outlet revenue, passenger traffic, flight schedules, weather conditions, and destination airport coordinates. The objective is to identify revenue drivers for stores in the departure zone and build a predictive model.

## Architecture

A Streamlit application organized as a sequential analytical pipeline of six stages:

1. **Data familiarization.** Source datasets: revenue in 30-minute intervals, passenger traffic logs (flights, terminals, airlines), schedules with aircraft types, airport and airline reference tables, METAR archive.

2. **EDA.** A series of interactive Plotly charts: passenger traffic distribution by airline and terminal, flight rankings by load, top outlets by revenue, dynamics by hour and day. Each chart is accompanied by an analytical commentary.

3. **Hypotheses and data enrichment.** Merging 7 sources into a single table: weather parameters, passenger traffic, temporal features (hour, day of week, weekend), geodata. Distances from SVO to destination airports are calculated using geodesic coordinates (Flightradar24 database). Four hypotheses are tested: the impact of weather, time of day, weekends, and flight distance.

4. **Model training.** CatBoost regression with time-series cross-validation (TimeSeriesSplit), preventing future data leakage. Three variants: a model on enriched data, a model on raw data, and a naive baseline (previous interval revenue). Metrics: RMSE, MAE, R2.

5. **Verification of conclusions.** The student determines which hypotheses were confirmed. Result: three out of four -- weather (passenger delays in the zone), time of day, and day of the week affect revenue; flight distance does not.

6. **Quiz.** Seven questions on the case material.

## Features

- Full DS cycle: EDA, feature engineering, ML model, business recommendations
- CatBoost regression for forecasting 30-minute revenue
- Merging 7 data sources with geospatial enrichment
- 10+ interactive Plotly visualizations with analytical commentaries
- Interactive knowledge assessment quiz

**ML:** CatBoost 1.1, scikit-learn 1.2 (TimeSeriesSplit)

**Data and Visualization:** pandas 1.5, numpy 1.23, plotly 5.11, geopy 2.3

**Deployment:** Docker Compose
