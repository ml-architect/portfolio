## About the Project

A collection of 5 hackathon solutions from "Digital Breakthrough" — an all-Russian competition for regional digitalization. Each project solves a real-world problem for a specific region of Russia.

## Projects

### Detection of Illegal Buildings via Satellite Imagery
**Region:** Udmurt Republic
Analysis of satellite imagery for automatic detection of illegal buildings. Computer Vision + Satellite imagery.

### Classification of Citizen Appeals
**Region:** Kursk Oblast
Automatic classification of citizen text appeals by category. NLP + Text classification.

### Municipal Services Optimization
**Region:** Republic of Bashkortostan
Optimization of municipal service routes and schedules. Operations Research.

### Museum Exhibit Search
**Region:** Lipetsk
Search for similar museum exhibits by image. Information Retrieval + Computer Vision.

### Library Recommendation System
**Region:** Tyumen
Personalized book recommendations for library visitors. RecSys.

## Key Achievements

- **5 real-world cases** for the public sector across different regions
- **Breadth of expertise** — CV, NLP, RecSys, Optimization in a single repository
- **Satellite imagery** — a rare and in-demand skill
- **Socially significant tasks** — digitalization of Russian regions
